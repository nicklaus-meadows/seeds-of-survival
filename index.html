Growing Security for a Growing World.

Seeds of Survival

Seed of Survival is an exhibition that aims to inform the community about our food, where it comes from, the impact transportation has on the earth, and the vulnerabilities we set ourselves up for relying on brittle supply chains. We hope to offer knowledge, support, and education on how everyone can make a positive impact on the earth we all share but also protect their families at the same time.


Through the exhibit we can learn about our own personal impacts on the environment and how to make a change for a better future.

Key Takeaways

  • Learn About Food Miles
  • Learn About Carbon Footprint
  • Learn About Local Food
  • Learn About Growing Your Own Food
  • Learn About Preserving Your Own Food
